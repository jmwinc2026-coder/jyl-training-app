Standard Draw Procedure
Step 1/7
1
Verify Patient Identity
Ask the patient to state their full name and date of birth. Cross-reference against the requisition order. Never rely solely on room number or wristband without verbal confirmation. Identity errors are one of the most common — and most preventable — mistakes in phlebotomy.
📷 Procedure image uploads here
☘ Pro Tip
Always use two patient identifiers per Joint Commission standards — name AND date of birth are the gold standard pair.
